# QuickChat — Member Guide

QuickChat is your server's AI support assistant. It has read through the server's help channel history and can answer your questions instantly — no need to wait for an admin.

---

## How to Ask a Question

Just **@mention the bot** followed by your question in any channel:

```
@QuickChat how do I reset my password?
```

```
@QuickChat where can I find the dashboard?
```

```
@QuickChat what are the server rules?
```

The bot will reply directly to your message with an answer pulled from real past conversations in this server.

---

## What Kind of Questions Can I Ask?

QuickChat works best for questions that have been asked and answered before in this server's help channel. Good examples:

- How-to questions about the server or its tools
- Questions about rules, roles, or permissions
- Common troubleshooting steps
- Anything a support admin has explained before

It won't know about things that have never been discussed in the server's help channel.

---

## Tips for Better Answers

- **Ask one question at a time** — the bot focuses on a single question per message
- **Be specific** — instead of *"help"*, try *"how do I verify my account?"*
- **Use natural language** — no need for special commands or keywords, just ask normally
- **Include context if needed** — e.g. *"I'm on mobile, how do I change my nickname?"*

---

## When the Bot Says It Doesn't Know

If the bot replies with:

> *"I couldn't find the answer to this in the server history. Could a human admin step in and help out?"*

It means no relevant past conversation was found for your question. Here's what to do:

1. **Tag an admin** or post in the appropriate support channel
2. When an admin answers your question, that answer gets stored
3. Next time someone asks the same thing, the bot will know the answer

So your question actually helps everyone — the bot gets smarter every time an admin answers something new!

---

## What the Bot Can and Can't Do

| ✅ Can do | ❌ Can't do |
|---|---|
| Answer questions based on server history | Answer questions never discussed here |
| Reply instantly at any time | Perform actions (kick, ban, assign roles) |
| Learn from new admin answers over time | Browse the internet or external sources |
| Handle multiple questions from different users | Remember your previous conversations |

---

## Privacy

- QuickChat only reads the server's designated help channel
- It does **not** read your DMs or any other channels
- It does **not** store your username or personal information — only the content of Q&A pairs from the help channel

---

## FAQ

**Q: Can I use the bot in any channel?**
Yes. You can @mention QuickChat in any channel you have access to and it will reply there.

**Q: Why did the bot give a wrong or outdated answer?**
The bot answers based on past conversations. If something has changed since then, the old answer may still be in its knowledge. Let an admin know so they can provide an updated answer using Discord Reply — the bot will learn the new one.

**Q: Can I ask follow-up questions?**
The bot treats each @mention as a fresh question. It doesn't remember what you asked before, so include all the context you need in each message.

**Q: The bot isn't responding — what do I do?**
Make sure you're @mentioning it correctly (e.g. `@QuickChat`). If it still doesn't respond, the bot may be offline — contact an admin.

**Q: Can I talk to the bot in DMs?**
No. QuickChat only works inside the server.
